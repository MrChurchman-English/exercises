Explanations

Question 1: bring in new clients

"Bring in" means to attract or obtain something, especially customers or business. This is a common business phrase.

Question 2: get by financially

"Get by" means to manage to survive or cope, especially financially with limited resources.

Question 3: take her on

"Take someone on" means to hire or employ someone for a job or position.

Question 4: take off

"Take off" means to start to improve rapidly or become successful (referring to her career).

Question 5: put up with

"Put up with" means to tolerate or endure something unpleasant or difficult.

Question 6: face up to

"Face up to" means to accept and deal with something difficult or unpleasant.

Question 7: turn down

"Turn down" means to refuse or reject an offer, invitation, or opportunity.

Question 8: bring up the issue

"Bring up" means to mention or raise a topic in conversation.
